ServiceCard — plain card for a legal service line item, marked with the § (section-sign) glyph in place of an icon.

```jsx
<ServiceCard title="Tipos de Arraigo">
  Social, Laboral, Familiar o para la Formación...
</ServiceCard>
```

Used in a 2-column grid on the homepage services section. No icon system beyond the § glyph — do not substitute a different icon here.
