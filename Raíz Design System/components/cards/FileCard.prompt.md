FileCard — the signature "case file" card from the homepage pain-points section: a torn-paper card that stamps a bureaucratic problem REJECTED, then stamps Raíz's fix APPROVED below a dashed divider.

```jsx
<FileCard
  tabLabel="Expediente 01"
  problemTitle="Citas Imposibles"
  problemText="Conseguir cita en la Oficina de Extranjería en Vigo es frustrante..."
  solutionText="Presentamos por Mercurio, vía telemática, al instante."
/>
```

Always used in a 3-up grid on the homepage. Composes the Stamp badge internally — don't nest a separate Stamp inside it.
