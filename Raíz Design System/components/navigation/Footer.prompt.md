Footer — dark (ink-colored) site footer with wordmark, two nav columns and the mandatory administrative-agency legal disclaimer (Raíz is not a licensed law firm).

```jsx
<Footer
  navLinks={[{label:'Servicios',href:'#servicios'},{label:'Blog',href:'blog.html'}]}
  legalLinks={[{label:'Aviso Legal',href:'#'},{label:'Política de Privacidad',href:'#'}]}
/>
```

Never drop the legal disclaimer text/column — it is a regulatory requirement for this business model (see BUSINESS_MODEL_CANVAS.md).
